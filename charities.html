American Red Cross

The American Red Cross is recognized for its widespread impact, supported by volunteers, donors, and partnerships. Its commitment to responding to emergencies and aiding communities in times of need has solidified its reputation as a vital humanitarian organization on both national and international scales.

The Children Foundation

The Charity Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting various social causes globally. Committed to making a positive impact, they focus on initiatives such as education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, poverty alleviation, and community development.

Living Hope Foundation

The Living Hope Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing support, resources, and hope to individuals and families affected by terminal illnesses, especially cancer. Founded on the belief that every person battling a terminal illness deserves comfort and assistance, the foundation offers various services.
